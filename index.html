About Us

We're a group of desis who live for karaoke, trivia nights, and hosting game nights with our friends and family. But every time we went to mainstream trivia events, we felt something was missing — where were the Bollywood bangers, the filmi drama, the inside jokes only desi folks get?

That's why we started Hungama Nights — to bring that same energy to our community, in a way that celebrates who we are.

We're here for everyone who grew up quoting movies in Hindi, singing in Telugu on road trips, watching Tamil or Kannada songs on TV, or cracking jokes in a mix of five languages. We wanted to create something that's not just Bollywood-focused, but truly inclusive of all Indian languages and cultures — because our stories, songs, and sass deserve a stage too.

Whether you're a trivia buff, karaoke star, or just in it for the tambola prizes and masala vibes — there's a seat for you at the table. Come hungry, come loud, and come ready for full on masti.
